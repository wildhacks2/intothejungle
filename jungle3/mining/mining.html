Mining is the process of obtaining coal or other minerals from a mine. The following are some of the effects

1. Deforestation

Wood from surrounding forest is cut for charocal to fuel mining processes. Species lose their habitat. Endemic species unable to survive anywhere else. Thus, population dwindle and will eventually become extinct.



2. Pollution

Run-off from mine will pollute surrounding water which will threaten both the species living in it and species that feed on it. Heavy metals will be present in run-off which is poisonous to animals. Animals consume the water or animals in the water (e.g. fishes), will then die due to being poisoned. May lead to extinction of species.
