Oil and Gas Extraction is the exploration and production of petroleum and natural gas from wells. The following are some of the effects

1. Deforestation

Opening up corridors for the pipeline alters draining patterns, affects movement of species, interrupts seed dispersal and natural forest regeneration, destroys habitat and negatively impacts the landscape.



2. Pollution

Waate from extraction activities will pollute and poison nearby water bodies. Animals consume the water or animals in the water (e.g. fishes), will then die due to being poisoned. May lead to extinction of species.



2. Fish Stock Declines

Pipeline's construction along steep ridges results in severe soil erosion and landslides which can wipe out fish population. Animals that solely feed on these fishes will starve and die off.
